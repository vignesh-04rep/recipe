Here's a simple recipe for pulikuzambu, a popular South Indian lentil soup:

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup tamarind pulp
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons sambar powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Allow them to splutter.
  2. Add fenugreek seeds and curry leaves. Sauté for a few seconds.
  3. Add chopped onion and sauté until it turns golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft.
  5. Add sambar powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add tamarind pulp and water to adjust the consistency.
  7. Cook on medium heat for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Remove from heat and serve Pulikuzambu with rice or idli.
